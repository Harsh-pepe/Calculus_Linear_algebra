The unified story

Linear algebra gives you the language of data — vectors, matrices, transformations, and dimensionality reduction. Calculus gives you the language of change — derivatives, integrals, and gradients. Together, they power every modern AI and ML system. Data enters as a matrix, passes through linear transformations, a loss function is computed, its gradient is calculated, gradient descent updates the model, and PCA compresses the result. You haven't learned 11 isolated topics — you've learned one continuous story.
